Multiple Choice

1. What is a solar flare?

  1. A bright explosion of energy from the Sun
  2. A storm on Earth
  3. A type of satellite
  4. A comet passing by Earth
Show Answer

Answer: a) A bright explosion of energy from the Sun

2. What does CME stand for?

  1. Cosmic Magnetic Energy
  2. Coronal Mass Ejection
  3. Celestial Motion Event
  4. Comet Meteor Explosion
Show Answer

Answer: b) Coronal Mass Ejection

3. What can happen to satellites when a solar storm reaches Earth?

  1. They glow with auroras
  2. They can get pushed out of orbit or disrupted
  3. They explode automatically
  4. Nothing happens
Show Answer

Answer: b) They can get pushed out of orbit or disrupted

4. Which of these can be caused by space weather?

  1. Auroras in the sky
  2. GPS signal disruptions
  3. Power outages
  4. All of the above
Show Answer

Answer: d) All of the above

5. Who watches the Sun and solar storms to protect Earth?

  1. Pilots
  2. Farmers
  3. Scientists and satellites
  4. Firefighters
Show Answer

Answer: c) Scientists and satellites

6. What does Flara, the solar flare, usually create when she reaches Earth?

Answer: b) Auroras and energy that can affect satellites and radios
